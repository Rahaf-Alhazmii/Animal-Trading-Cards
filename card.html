Morpho

Morphos have a very distinctive, slow, bouncy flight pattern due to the wing area being enormous relative to the body size.

  • Kingdom: Animalia
  • Phylum: Arthropoda
  • Class: Insecta
  • Order: Lepidoptera
  • Family: Nymphalidae
  • Tribe: Morphini

Morpho wingspans range from 7.5 cm to 20 cm. The name morpho meaning "changed" or "modified", is also an epithet. Morphos are diurnal, as males spend the mornings patrolling along the courses of forest streams and rivers. They are territorial and chase any rivals. Morphos typically live alone, excluding in the mating season.
